Send Custom Messages

Sample: If your Excel file contains columns with headers like `number`, `name`, and `balance`, you can customize your message accordingly. For example, you might write:
Dear @name, your balance is @balance. This message will be sent to all the [numbers] listed in your Excel file.

Why Custom Messages? Custom messages allow you to personalize bulk messages, making them more relevant and engaging for recipients. This can lead to better customer relationships and improved communication.

Step 1 - Upload the Excel File Step 2 - Type Your Message Step 3 - View Results

File Format: Ensure your Excel file is properly formatted with headers like `number`, `name`, and `balance` in the first row. Each row beneath should contain the corresponding data for each recipient.

Uploading: Click the browse button to locate your Excel file on your device, then click "Upload File" to proceed.

Tip: Make sure your file is in .xlsx or .xls format for smooth processing.

Compose Your Message: Type your custom message in the text area below, using placeholders like @name or @balance as needed. These placeholders will be replaced with the actual data from your Excel file.

Example: "Dear @name, your balance is @balance. Please clear your balance by @date."

Results: After sending the messages, you will receive a detailed report on the success of your campaign, including any errors or undelivered messages. Make sure to review this report to ensure your messages reached the intended recipients.
